Casual Outfit Suggestions

Based on your selections, here are some outfit suggestions for a casual day out:

For Men:

  1. Classic Everyday Look
    • T-shirt (solid or minimal print)
    • Jeans (slim or straight fit)
    • Sneakers
  2. Smart Casual Vibe
    • Polo T-shirt or casual shirt
    • Chinos
    • Loafers or clean sneakers
  3. Layered Street Style
    • Basic T-shirt
    • Open shirt / flannel / light jacket
    • Jeans or cargo pants
    • Sneakers
  4. Athleisure Look
    • Hoodie or sweatshirt
    • Joggers
    • Sports shoes
  5. Minimalist Casual
    • Solid color sweater
    • Dark jeans or trousers
    • Watch + simple sneakers

🌸 For Women

  1. Sweater + Jeans Look
    • Soft knit sweater
    • High-waist jeans
    • Sneakers or ankle boots
  2. Oversized Fit Style
    • Oversized sweatshirt
    • Straight-leg jeans or joggers
    • Sneakers
  3. Casual Kurti Fusion
    • Short kurti + jeans
    • Sneakers or flats
    • Minimal accessories
  4. Denim-on-Denim
    • Denim jacket + jeans
    • Plain top inside
    • White shoes
  5. Street Style Look
    • Crop top
    • Cargo pants
    • Sneakers
