❄️ Winter College Outfit Suggestions

Based on your selections, here are some outfit suggestions for a casual day out:

🧥 For Men:

  1. Classic College Layering
    • T-shirt + hoodie + jacket
    • Jeans or chinos
    • Sneakers
  2. Hoodie + Puffer Jacket
    • Thick hoodie
    • Puffer jacket
    • Slim-fit jeans
    • Sports shoes
  3. Sweater + Shirt Combo
    • Collared shirt inside
    • Crewneck sweater on top
    • Trousers or jeans
    • Sneakers
  4. Street Style Winter Fit
    • Oversized sweatshirt
    • Cargo pants
    • High-top sneakers
    • Beanie or cap
  5. Minimal Clean Look
    • Turtleneck
    • Wool coat or simple jacket
    • Dark jeans
    • Leather shoes or boots

🌸 For Women

  1. Sweater + Jeans Look
    • Soft knit sweater
    • High-waist jeans
    • Sneakers or ankle boots
  2. Hoodie + Long Coat
    • Hoodie inside
    • Long coat or overcoat
    • Straight pants
    • Sneakers
  3. Oversized Sweatshirt Fit
    • Oversized sweatshirt
    • Joggers or wide-leg pants
    • Chunky sneakers
  4. Kurti + Jacket Fusion
    • Kurti + leggings/jeans
    • Denim or wool jacket
    • Boots or flats
  5. Turtleneck + Skirt (with layers)
    • Turtleneck top
    • Skirt + leggings/stockings
    • Boots
