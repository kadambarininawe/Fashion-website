Summer College Outfit Suggestions

Based on your selections, here are some outfit suggestions for a summer college day:

For Men:

  1. Light Everyday Look
    • Cotton T-shirt (light colors)
    • Lightweight jeans or chinos
    • Sneakers
  2. Casual Smart Look
    • Polo T-shirt or half-sleeve shirt
    • Chinos
    • Loafers or sneakers
  3. Street Style Look
    • Oversized T-shirt
    • Cargo pants
    • Chunky sneakers
  4. Athleisure Style
    • Breathable T-shirt
    • Joggers
    • Sports shoes
  5. Minimal Look
    • Plain T-shirt
    • Dark trousers
    • White sneakers

🌸 For Women

  1. Simple College Look
    • Cotton top
    • High-waist jeans
    • Sneakers or flats
  2. Cool Casual Look
    • T-shirt + wide-leg pants
    • Light colors
    • Sneakers
  3. Kurti Casual Look
    • Short kurti + jeans
    • Comfortable flats
    • Minimal accessories
  4. Street Style Look
    • Crop top
    • Cargo pants
    • Sneakers
  5. Denim Style
    • Denim skirt or jeans
    • Light top
    • White shoes
